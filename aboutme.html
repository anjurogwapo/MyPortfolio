ABOUT ME

Hey there! I’m Chim Anjuro
a creative spirit who loves turning ideas into reality. I’m a model, artist, UI/UX designer, and graphic designer, plus the proud owner of Anjuro Clothing, a brand I started on Instagram back in 2020 to share my love for fashion and creativity with the world.
I’m a Filipino college student, currently in my third year at the University of St. La Salle, studying BSIT. Creativity has always been a huge part of who I am, whether it’s designing, modeling, or just diving into new artistic challenges. I draw inspiration from my culture, my experiences, and the people around me.

MY GOAL

My goal is simple: to create. Whether it’s through design, art, or storytelling, I want my work to leave an impression, inspire others, and make a difference.
When you work with me, you’re getting more than just a creative hand—you’re teaming up with someone who genuinely loves what they do. I bring passion, dedication, and a fresh perspective to every project. Let’s make something amazing together!
